Debloating

Debloating techniques currently in place include but are not limited to:

  • Vastly reduced TXQUEUELEN accoss all ethernet and wireless interfaces.
  • Reduced buffering in the ethernet switch
  • Reduced driver buffering accoss all ethernet and wireless interfaces
  • QoS enabled by default
